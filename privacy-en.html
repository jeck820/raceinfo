Privacy Policy — RaceInfo

Last updated: August 10, 2025

1. Data Collection

RaceInfo does not collect, store, or share personal user data. All information remains solely on the user’s device, except for what is strictly necessary for the services used.

2. In-App Purchases

The app uses RevenueCat to manage and verify in-app purchases. RevenueCat may process limited information (such as anonymous user IDs, purchase history, and subscription type) solely to ensure proper purchase functionality. For more details, please see RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party API Data Access

RaceInfo uses the API-Sports service (api-sports.io) to retrieve Formula 1 data (calendars, schedules, results, etc.). When the app makes requests to the API, the service may receive technical information such as IP address, device/app type, and system logs necessary for its operation. These data are handled according to the API-Sports Privacy Policy.

4. Security

RaceInfo does not use advertising trackers or invasive analytics tools.
